# Personal Contribution

## My role

I worked mainly on Power BI prototyping and the reporting workflow around forecasting outputs.

My contribution included:

- developing an early Power BI dashboard prototype
- helping connect structured user inputs with forecasting outputs for reporting review
- working with Excel-based inputs used by the reporting workflow
- checking data-loading behaviour and helping resolve a refresh issue traced to the input layer
- presenting the reporting workflow and project recommendations
- contributing to documentation and handover thinking

## How I worked with the team

The wider solution was produced by a project team. Other team members contributed to forecasting models, optimisation logic, project management, documentation, and related technical components.

My work depended on those shared components. My responsibility was to help turn technical outputs into a reporting view that could be reviewed and discussed more easily.

## What I learned

The project strengthened my understanding that dashboard work is not only visual design. Reliable reporting also depends on:

- clear input structure
- traceable assumptions
- suitable data-loading logic
- careful interpretation of outputs
- documentation that supports handover
- regular communication between technical contributors

## Claim boundary

I do not claim that I independently developed the full forecasting or optimisation solution. I also do not claim production deployment, quantified business impact, or ownership of confidential team deliverables.
